H.O.P.E. - Wellcome Trust proposal

Human Optimised Pluripotent stem cell Engineering

Test: Humanity is dying. Advanced medical technology wiped out disease – we thought – but in the process left our immune systems withered and untested.

When the technology failed, the impact was catastrophic. Millions lay dead in the streets, felled by pathogens not seen since the dark ages of the 21st century.

Only one option remains: a new technique known as Human Optimised Pluripotent stem cell Engineering. As a H.O.P.E. technician, your job is to build a new immune system for patients using a single stem cell.

Mankind's survival rests on a single cell. And you control it.
